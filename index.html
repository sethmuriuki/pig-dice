Pig Dice

Rules:

1)If a player rolls 1 they score 0 and lose their turn

2)If a player rolls any other number other than 1 it is added to
their turn total and the player continues

3)If a player chooses to hold, their turn total is added to their score
and the next player gets a turn

4)The first player to score 100 points wins
